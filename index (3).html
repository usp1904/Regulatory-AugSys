Regulatory Delivery Assurance
MARAS is a regulated business value engine
MARAS is evaluated as a regulator-defensible delivery layer: source truth first, then business requirement, control intent, risk, PBI, test obligation, evidence and inspection readiness. Jira work items are only one export from the package.
1. Source of Truth Validation
Official source, license posture, and usage rule
Production rule: every ingested regulatory document must carry official URL, authority, document class, effective date, capture date, file hash, license tag, and SME approval before it is allowed to drive generated outputs. This demo includes source metadata and guardrails; live re-validation and hashing require a connected ingestion service.

AI work generation layer

Drafts work items, summarizes context, and accelerates team execution. In regulated delivery this still leaves source validation, interpretation, control mapping, test obligation, evidence packaging and inspection readiness as manual work.

MARAS business assurance layer

Converts a regulation or SOP into business-specific controls, PBIs, risk rationale, test obligations, evidence gaps, approval records and productivity metrics. The sellable value is a defensible package that reduces compliance cycle time and rework.
